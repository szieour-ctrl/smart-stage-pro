5. AB 723 Compliance Data — Special Retention Requirements

California Assembly Bill 723 (effective January 1, 2026) requires that virtual staging disclosures and original photographs be retained and accessible. Smart Stage PRO™ maintains the following data as a permanent compliance record:

  • Original unaltered photographs for each staged image
  • The verbatim AI staging prompt used to generate each staged image
  • The date and time each staged image was generated
  • The agent's name and DRE license number
  • The property address
  • Public compliance page URL (permanent public record)

This data is retained for a minimum of 3 years from project creation date, in accordance with California DRE record retention requirements. Compliance pages remain publicly accessible at their permanent URL even after a listing is archived or a subscription is cancelled. If a subscription is cancelled, the agent of record will receive a data archive via email within 30 days.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in the United States on infrastructure provided by Supabase (PostgreSQL), Cloudinary, Netlify, and Railway (video render pipeline)
  • Database access is protected by row-level security (RLS) — agents can only access their own listings and compliance data
  • API keys and service credentials are stored as server-side environment variables and are never exposed to the browser
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth — we never store or transmit plaintext passwords
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Payment data never touches our servers — it is tokenized by Stripe at the point of entry

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained for the duration of the subscription
  • AB 723 compliance records: Minimum 3 years from project creation, regardless of subscription status
  • Cancelled subscriptions: Non-compliance account data deleted after 90 days; compliance records retained per above
  • Help Agent conversations: Not retained beyond the browser session
  • Stripe billing records: Retained per Stripe's data retention policy

8. Your Rights (California Residents — CCPA)

As a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to AB 723 compliance retention requirements
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information — there is nothing to opt out of
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@smartstagepro.com. We will respond within 45 days. Note that AB 723 compliance records cannot be deleted regardless of deletion requests, as they are required by California law.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Smart Stage PRO™ uses browser session storage and authentication tokens (managed by Supabase Auth) to maintain your logged-in session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not use Google Analytics or similar behavioral tracking services.

The platform uses a Supabase authentication token stored in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in. This token expires automatically and can be cleared by signing out.

10. Children's Privacy

Smart Stage PRO™ is a professional platform intended for licensed real estate agents and brokers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at support@smartstagepro.com.
